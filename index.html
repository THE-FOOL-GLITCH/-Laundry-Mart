Our professional services

  • Wash-and-Fold Service: Convenient, bulk laundry processing where clothes are washed, dried, and neatly folded.
  • Dry Cleaning: Professional cleaning for delicate or specialized garments like suits, dresses, and formal wear.
  • Ironing and Pressing: Expert finishing services to remove wrinkles and give clothing a crisp, polished look.
  • Pickup and Delivery: Door-to-door service for maximum customer convenience, scheduled via web or app.
  • Commercial Laundry: Dedicated services for businesses (e.g., restaurants, hotels, gyms) requiring high-volume linen and uniform cleaning.
  • Specialty Item Cleaning: Care for large, difficult-to-clean items like comforters, duvets, rugs, and curtains.
  • Stain Treatment: Targeted pre-treatment and specialized techniques to remove stubborn spots and stains.
  • Alterations and Repairs: Simple clothing repairs like hemming, button replacement, and minor mending (often offered alongside dry cleaning).
